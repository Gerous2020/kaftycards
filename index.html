What is a Digital Visiting Card?

A Digital Visiting Card (or mini-website) is a modern replacement for traditional paper business cards. It lives on your phone and can be shared instantly with anyone, anywhere.

Always in Pocket

Never run out of cards. It's always on your phone.

Clickable Links

Clients can call, WhatsApp, or email you with just one click.

Why Switch to Digital?

Paper cards are expensive, bad for the environment, and often get thrown away. In the digital age, sharing a paper card feels outdated.

Paper Cards

  • ❌ Easy to lose
  • ❌ Expensive to print
  • ❌ Limited information
  • ❌ Hard to update details

Digital Cards

  • ✅ Impossible to lose
  • ✅ Zero printing cost
  • ✅ Unlimited photos/videos
  • ✅ Update anytime for free
